How AuraScore Works

Track your aura. Beat your friends. Level up every day.

1 Your Daily Aura Score

Every day you get a score from 0 to 99. Your score is based on 4 traits: Confidence, Style, Discipline, and Social Energy. The higher your score, the stronger your aura. Each trait feeds into your overall number — so working on all four is how you peak.

2 The 4 Aura Traits

Confidence
Style
Discipline
Social Energy

Each trait is scored independently. You can be elite in one and mid in another — that's the game. Every challenge you complete shifts your traits. Some boost Confidence, some boost Discipline. Pay attention to what each one rewards.

3 Daily Challenges

Every day you get a new Aura Challenge. Complete it and you earn aura points. Skip it and your score stays flat or drops. Challenges are things like: post a clean fit, finish your workout, make one strong social move, or hit a discipline goal. Simple. Real. No fluff.

4 Streaks and Momentum

The longer you keep your streak alive, the faster your aura builds. Miss a day and the streak resets. Your trend indicator — Ascending, Surging, or Stalling — shows the direction you're moving in. Momentum is everything. Don't break the chain.

5 The League Board

You are always competing. Your score is ranked live against your friends. The league updates daily. Rankings shift based on who's showing up and who's falling off. There's no hiding — either you're putting in the work or you're not.

6 Levels

Your level goes up as your total aura accumulates over time. Level 1 is just getting started. Level 99 is untouchable. Your level is permanent — even if your daily score dips, you keep the levels you've earned. This is your long-term status.

7 Recalculate Aura

Hit Recalculate Aura to get a fresh daily read on your score based on current energy inputs. Use it once per day to check in. It's your honest mirror — not what you wish your aura was, what it actually is right now.
